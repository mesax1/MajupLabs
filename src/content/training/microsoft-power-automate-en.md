## The Power of the Microsoft Ecosystem

If your company uses Microsoft 365, you have access to enterprise-class automation tools. Power Automate and Copilot transform the way you work.

## Why Power Automate?

Microsoft has invested heavily in its automation platform:

- **Native integration** with the entire Microsoft 365 ecosystem
- **RPA included** to automate desktop applications
- **AI Builder** for no-code AI
- **Copilot** for AI-assisted productivity
- **Enterprise-ready** with governance and security

## What You'll Learn

### Power Automate Cloud

Create flows that connect your Microsoft 365 applications. From simple automations to complex business processes with multiple approvers.

### Power Automate Desktop

Microsoft's robotic process automation (RPA). Automate desktop applications, even those without an API, by recording your actions.

### Microsoft Copilot

Microsoft's AI integrated into your work tools. Learn to get the most out of it in Excel, Word, Outlook, and Teams.

### AI Builder

Create artificial intelligence models without writing code. Document processing, prediction, classification, and more.

## Systems You'll Build

| System | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Document Processor** | Extract data from invoices and contracts with AI Builder |
| **Approval Workflow** | Multi-level approval flow with notifications |
| **SharePoint Automation** | Automated document and permission management |
| **Teams Bot** | Bot that answers questions and executes tasks |
| **Legacy App Bridge** | Connect old applications with the cloud via RPA |

## Methodology

Practical and progressive approach:

1. **Foundation** (15 min): Concept and use cases
2. **Demonstration** (20 min): Step-by-step building
3. **Practice** (45 min): Your turn to build
4. **Real Case** (10 min): Application to your context

## Prerequisites

- Access to Microsoft 365 (business license or higher)
- Familiarity with Excel, Outlook, and Teams
- No programming experience required
- Power Automate Premium license for some features
