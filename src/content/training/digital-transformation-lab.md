## Experiment, Build, Transform

The Digital Transformation Lab is an experiential program designed for teams that want to go beyond theory. Here you don't just learn about AIâ€”you apply it directly to your company's real challenges.

## What is the Lab?

It's a practical experimentation space where your team:

- **Identifies** automation and AI opportunities in their processes
- **Builds** functional solutions during sessions
- **Validates** impact with real metrics from your business
- **Implements** the most effective solutions in production

Unlike traditional courses, each lab session produces **actionable deliverables** your team can use immediately.

## Program Structure

### Phase 1: Diagnosis and Quick Wins

We start by mapping your current processes and identifying where AI can generate immediate value. During this phase:

- We analyze existing workflows
- We identify repetitive, high-volume tasks
- We prioritize opportunities by impact and feasibility
- We define the first pilot projects

### Phase 2: Practical Labs

Each lab focuses on building a specific solution:

**Automation Lab**
Learn to connect your tools and eliminate manual tasks. We work with platforms like N8N, Google Apps Script, Power Automate, and more.

**Intelligent Agents Lab**
Build chatbots and agents that answer questions, process requests, and execute tasks semi-autonomously.

**Data and Insights Lab**
Transform scattered data into actionable information with dashboards, automatic reports, and AI-powered analysis.

### Phase 3: Implementation and Scaling

We don't leave solutions as prototypes. We accompany you in:

- Production deployment
- Documentation and knowledge transfer
- Maintenance and evolution plan
- Identification of next projects

## Systems You'll Build

| System | Benefit |

## Lab Tools

Throughout the program you'll experiment with:

- **N8N**: Workflow automation
- **AI APIs**: OpenAI, Anthropic, Google AI
- **Google Workspace**: Apps Script, Sheets, Gmail
- **Microsoft 365**: Power Automate, Copilot, SharePoint
- **Scraping tools**: Intelligent web data extraction
- **Vector databases**: For semantic search

## Who is the Lab For?

This program is designed for **complete teams**, not individuals. The idea is that knowledge stays within the organization and projects have clear owners.

It works especially well for:

- Teams already using digital tools but wanting to optimize
- Organizations undergoing digital transformation
- Companies wanting to reduce dependency on external developers
- Areas with many manual and repetitive tasks

## Working Methodology

Each lab session follows this structure:

1. **Context** (15 min): Key concepts and success stories
2. **Demonstration** (20 min): Facilitator builds live
3. **Guided Practice** (60 min): Your team builds with support
4. **Application** (25 min): Adaptation to your specific case
5. **Closure** (10 min): Next steps and assignments

## Program Deliverables

Upon completion, your team will have:

- Minimum 3 automations running in production
- A chatbot or AI agent configured for your business
- Documentation of automated processes
- Roadmap for next AI projects
- Trained team to maintain and extend solutions
