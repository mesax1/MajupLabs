## Our Methodology

Our bootcamps follow an agile, adaptable, results-focused structure:

### 1. Diagnosis and Opportunity Identification

We start with a context analysis to detect strategic challenges where generative AI can create real value for your organization. We use advanced technology monitoring techniques to identify trends and opportunities through analytical tools.

### 2. Building Solutions with AI

Through practical dynamics, your team builds functional prototypes in real-time using generative AI tools and techniques. We work with real cases from your company to maximize applicability.

### 3. Validation, Adjustment, and Strategic Closure

We validate the results obtained, optimize, and leave a clear plan of next steps for implementation. All this in agile sessions, guided by expert facilitators, adapted to your company's pace and reality.
